July 9, 2026
New

CMO Agent: Full Marketing Leadership in a Governed Agent

The AtlaSynq CMO Agent is now generally available for all Enterprise plans. It operates at the level of a senior GTM leader — running GA4 analytics reports, building AEO content briefs, generating SEO keyword strategies, publishing blog articles through the CMO publish pipeline, and creating quarterly OKRs.

New in this release:

  • Blog publish skill: CMO Agent can now generate and publish full SEO/AEO-optimized articles directly from a single prompt, routed through your CMO publish API with human review gates.
  • GA4 deep integration: Live acquisition, conversion, and page-level reports pulled directly from your Google Analytics 4 property.
  • AEO citation monitoring: Weekly monitoring of AI assistant citation frequency across target keywords.
  • Monday.com campaign push: Generated content plans and editorial calendars sync directly to your Monday.com marketing board.
June 25, 2026
Redesign

Clay UI: New Marketing Design System Launched

AtlaSynq's marketing site has been completely redesigned with our new clay design system — featuring Fraunces and Sora typography, a warm terracotta and cream color palette, and a tactile card aesthetic that reflects our brand's authenticity and warmth.

All marketing pages — including blog posts, product updates, the ROI calculator, and the pricing page — now use the shared clay-marketing.css design system for consistent brand presentation. The new design system is open to AtlaSynq customers building white-label agent interfaces.

June 15, 2026
GA

Marketing Agent: GA4 Analytics Integration Generally Available

The Marketing Agent's Google Analytics 4 integration is now generally available. Customers with Google Workspace connected can ask their Marketing Agent for live analytics data — no more downloading CSV exports or building Looker Studio dashboards.

Available GA4 skills:

  • Acquisition report: Source/medium/campaign traffic breakdown with bounce rate and session duration.
  • Conversion report: Key events and conversion rates by source and medium.
  • Pages report: Top landing pages by views, sessions, duration, and bounce rate.
  • Custom report: Any dimension × metric combination with custom date ranges.

All GA4 queries are logged in the AtlaSynq audit trail and respect your Google Workspace OAuth scopes — no analytics data leaves your authorized session.

Feature

Governed Connectors: 12 New Enterprise Integrations

AtlaSynq's connector library now includes 12 new enterprise integrations, all with full RBAC enforcement and audit logging:

  • Gong — Call recordings, deal insights, and coaching flags for Sales Agents
  • Cognism — B2B contact research and enrichment for Marketing and Sales Agents
  • Coralogix — Log intelligence and incident context for Admin Agents
  • NetSuite — ERP data access for Finance and Operations Agents
  • QuickBooks — Accounting data for Finance Agents (read-only by default)
  • LinkedIn — Profile lookups and company intel for Sales and Marketing Agents
  • And 6 more — see the integrations catalog for the full list.

All connectors enforce AtlaSynq's standard RBAC model: agent access is scoped to the authenticated user's role and workspace permissions.

May 20, 2026
New

ROI Calculator: Interactive Value Modeling for Enterprise Teams

AtlaSynq now ships an interactive ROI calculator at /roi/ — letting prospects and customers model the exact productivity savings and payback period for their team size, hourly cost, and current AI tool spend.

The calculator uses real methodology from our customer success team's onboarding assessments and outputs annual savings, payback period, and a department-level breakdown. Results can be shared directly with a Calendly demo booking link for immediate sales follow-up.

The calculator is available as a standalone page at /roi/ for sales conversations and paid media campaigns.
