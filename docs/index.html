What the platform does

Not a shared chatbot with plugins. A governance layer between your stack and your teams.

Connect once, scope by role

Admins authorize tools with OAuth. Each agent role — Sales, Finance, Engineering, HR — only sees the connectors and fields its policy allows.

Answers with sources

When someone asks a question, AtlaSynq retrieves from permitted systems and returns an answer tied to the exact record or document. No invented citations.

Approval before action

Read access is automatic within policy. Updates to CRM, tickets, or documents go to an admin queue first. Full audit trail on every request.

By department

Same platform, different tool boundaries per team. Details on the features page.

  • SalesPipeline status, account context, and follow-ups from CRM and call recordings — without finance or HR data.
  • FinanceRunway, variance, and vendor questions from ERP and accounting tools — scoped away from sales pipeline.
  • EngineeringRelease blockers, PR status, and incident context from Jira, GitHub, and observability — repo permissions respected.
  • HR & LegalPolicy lookup and contract questions from HRIS and document stores — with stricter write controls.

Security and compliance

SOC 2 Type II, SSO/SAML, encryption in transit and at rest, zero training on customer data. RBAC is checked at query time, not only at login. Enterprise plans support VPC and on-premises deployment.
