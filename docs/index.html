FAQ

Common questions

What is AtlaSynq?

AtlaSynq is an enterprise platform that deploys governed AI agents for every business department — Finance, Sales, Engineering, HR, Marketing and Legal. Each agent has access only to the tools and data its role permits, and every answer is cited and logged.

How does AtlaSynq enforce governance on AI agents?

AtlaSynq applies role-based access control (RBAC) policies to every AI agent. Admins define what data each role can read, what actions agents may take, and which integrations are permitted — enforced at query time, not just at login.

Which AI models does AtlaSynq support?

AtlaSynq supports GPT-4o, Claude 3.5, Gemini 1.5 Pro, Llama 3 (self-hosted), GitHub Copilot, and Perplexity. You can assign a different model per department or per role and switch without re-deploying connectors.

Which integrations does AtlaSynq support?

AtlaSynq connects to Salesforce, HubSpot, Jira, GitHub, Slack, QuickBooks, Notion, Google Drive, Stripe, Gong, LinkedIn, Zendesk, Greenhouse, NetSuite, Teams, Linear, DocuSign, and many more via OAuth and API. Enterprise plans include custom connector development.

Is AtlaSynq secure for enterprise use?

AtlaSynq is SOC 2 Type II compliant, supports SSO/SAML, enforces end-to-end encryption in transit and at rest, and provides a full audit log for every agent action. We never train AI models on customer data.

How long does deployment take?

Teams are typically live within a week. You connect your tools via OAuth (no code required), configure role policies in the admin panel, and invite your team. Custom connectors or enterprise SSO setups typically add 1–2 weeks.
