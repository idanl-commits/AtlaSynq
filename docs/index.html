Frequently Asked Questions

Do you train on our data?

Absolutely not. AtlaSynq is a passthrough layer. Your data remains in your systems and is processed only to answer queries.

How are permissions handled?

We respect the RBAC (Role-Based Access Control) of the source system. If a user can't see it in Salesforce, they can't see it in AtlaSynq.

Can we deploy On-Premise?

Yes, for Enterprise plans we offer VPC peering and On-Prem deployment options.
