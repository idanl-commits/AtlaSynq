How We Protect Your Data

Every decision we made was driven by one principle: your data should never be at risk. Not in transit, not at rest, not ever.

Zero-Retention Architecture

We process queries in memory and discard them immediately. Your data never touches a persistent database.

Automatic Permission Mirroring

We read your existing RBAC from every system. If they can't see it in Salesforce, they can't see it here.

Complete Audit Trail

Every query, tool access, and response is logged with timestamps and user IDs. Full visibility, always.

Enterprise-Grade Security

We built security into every layer of our architecture, not as an afterthought.

Encryption at Rest & Transit

All data encrypted using industry-standard protocols. Even if intercepted, it remains unreadable.

On-Premise Deployment

Full VPC peering available. Your data never leaves your infrastructure.

No Third-Party Access

Continuous Monitoring
