FAQ

Pricing questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes. All plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. You get access to all features of your chosen plan so you can evaluate before committing.

What counts as a "user" for billing?

A user is any member of your workspace who can query an agent. Read-only observers and admin-only users (who configure policies but don't query agents) do not count toward the seat limit.

Can I switch plans mid-cycle?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes — annual billing saves 20% compared to monthly. Team at $39/user/mo and Business at $79/user/mo, billed annually. Contact sales for Enterprise annual pricing.

What integrations are included in the standard connectors?

Standard connectors include Salesforce, HubSpot, Jira, GitHub, Slack, QuickBooks, Notion, Google Drive, Stripe, Gong, LinkedIn, Zendesk, and more. Enterprise plans include custom connector development for any tool with an API.
