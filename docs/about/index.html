Zero-Retention by Design

Unlike competitors who store your data for "improvement," AtlaSynq processes queries in memory and discards everything immediately. Your data never touches a persistent database.

We don't train on your data, we don't cache it, and we don't log it. It's gone the moment the response is delivered.

Automatic Permission Mirroring

We read your existing RBAC from Salesforce, Jira, Slack, and every connected tool. No manual configuration.

If a Sales rep can't see a Finance record in Salesforce, they can't see it in AtlaSynq—even if they ask. The AI respects your existing security model automatically.

Universal Search, Not Siloed Queries

Ask "What's blocking the Acme Corp deal?" and AtlaSynq searches Salesforce opportunities, Slack threads, Jira tickets, and Drive documents in parallel—returning one unified answer with citations.

No switching between tools. No copy-pasting. One question, one complete answer.

Complete Audit Trail

Every query, every tool accessed, every permission checked, every response delivered—all logged with timestamps and user IDs.

Perfect for compliance reviews and security investigations. Know exactly who asked what, when, and what they got back.
